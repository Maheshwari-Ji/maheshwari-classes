About

Maheshwari Classes is an online coaching management application. This application helps managing Student, Teacher, Courses, Batches, Notes, Tests and Marks.

Batches

Teachers can add batches and include relevant courses in their batch.
Students can enroll in multiple batches as per their requirement.

Notes

Teachers add the notes of courses which are then available to the students for their better learning.

Tests

Students can submit the tests scheduled by the teachers and check their performance. The results and performances of all the students are available to the teachers.
